Our Founders

Arijanit Idrizi – President

Arijanit brings over 8 years of solid experience in the field of migration and socio-professional support. With a first training in business, he has successfully leveraged his skills in communication, behavioral analysis and problem-solving to thrive in the migration sector and as a freelance data analyst.

Drawing on this human-centered and strategic experience, he began a professional transition into the field of data science, with the aim of combining his social expertise and analytical skills to deliver innovative, data-driven solutions. Having faced several challenges during his own career change, he came up with the idea of creating an association to support others encountering similar obstacles.

Quôc-Bao Nguyên – Co-President

Quôc-Bao brings over ten years of experience as a social educator, with a strong focus on child protection, refugee support, and emergency housing. His work is grounded in a deep commitment to social justice and systemic change.

He has led projects for organizations like the Swiss Federal Department of Foreign Affairs (DFAE) and AdO, contributing to initiatives such as the One Million Youth Action Challenge (1MYAC).

With a Master’s in Social Work and Public Policy from the University of Fribourg, he bridges hands-on fieldwork with strategic thinking to influence public policy and promote equal access to services.

As Co-President of Sophira, he brings vision, experience, and a firm belief in social transformation through data and solidarity.
