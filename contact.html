Empowering Transitions with Purpose

Sophira is a non-profit initiative supporting career transitions through data science education, community, and solidarity.

The Challenge

Automation is disrupting the job market. Thousands of adults in Switzerland face unemployment or underemployment while lacking access to upskilling opportunities.

Our Mission

Our Vision

We envision a Switzerland where no one is left behind by digital transformation, and where career reinvention is possible at any age or background.
