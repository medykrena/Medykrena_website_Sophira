Empowering Transitions for Tomorrow’s Jobs

Sophira is a Swiss nonprofit supporting professional reconversion towards the jobs of the future, from data to green tech, for adults facing systemic barriers.

Why Sophira?

Sophira was born from a simple conviction: the digital and ecological transitions are disrupting today’s job market. Millions of adults, especially those with vulnerable paths, are at risk of exclusion.

We provide free education, human support, and career guidance to help them access new, meaningful jobs, by equipping them with digital, analytical, and future-proof skills.
