Empowering Transitions with Purpose

Sophira is a non-profit initiative supporting adults facing career disruption by guiding them toward future-proof professions through inclusive education, solidarity, and community.

Why Sophira?

Automation and economic transitions are redefining the job market, putting many professions at risk. Sophira was founded to support those who find themselves at a crossroads, especially adults facing economic hardship, social vulnerability, or structural barriers to training.

Our mission is to provide access to emerging fields, such as data science, green technology, digital innovation, and ethical AI—through grants, personalized support, and a strong community.

We believe that no one should be left behind in the digital transition. By promoting equal opportunities and inclusive career reinvention, Sophira helps build a fairer and more resilient society.

What Makes Us Different?

Sophira is not just a scholarship program, it’s a movement. We work with academic institutions, NGOs, and forward-thinking companies to create a supportive ecosystem. We also advocate for public policies that foster social mobility and digital inclusion.
