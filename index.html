Empowering Transitions with Purpose

Sophira is a non-profit initiative based in Switzerland, supporting adults in career transition through education, community, and opportunity.

Our Mission

In a rapidly evolving job market, many adults face the risk of professional exclusion. Sophira supports individuals who are motivated to transition into futur fields but are held back by financial barriers, isolation, or lack of guidance.

What We Offer

We provide targeted scholarships, coaching, community support, and access to digital tools. Our approach is participatory, inclusive, and designed to develop both technical skills and human potential.
